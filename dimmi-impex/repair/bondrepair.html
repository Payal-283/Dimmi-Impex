BOND REPAIR

SBR Latex for Repairs & Waterproofing

BOND REPAIR is a SBR based liquid useful as an additive for concrete / mortars in repair / waterproofing applications, as well as a protective coating to rebars and cementitious substrates. It also serves as a bond coat to repair sections.

Area of Application

  • Waterproofing of terrace, toilets, chajja, masonry walls, sloping RCC roofs, etc.
  • Repairs to RCC members
  • Waterproofing mortars / concretes
  • In repair mortars / concretes

    • Features & Benefits

  • White liquid, easily brush applied
  • Useful as waterproofing coat
  • Useful as a bonding agent between old and new concrete surfaces
  • Excellent as a cement modifier
  • Repair mortar modified with BOND REPAIR suitable for repairing of RCC beams, columns, slabs and other RCC members

    • Technical Information

  • Total solid content: 40 Â± 2%
  • Specific gravity: Approx. 1.00
  • pH: 8 - 9
  • Coverage: 20-22 sq.ft/kg for 2 coats. (BR + cement in the proportion 1:1.5)
  • Pot life: 30-45 minutes, when mixed with Cement at 30oC

    • Instructions: Precaution/Storage

  • Consume mixed material within 45 minutes
  • Moisten the surface adequately before coating application
  • Dilution of BOND REPAIR is strictly not recommended.
  • Do not apply coating over loose or cracked plaster surface
  • Not to be used for cathodic treatments to rebar
  • Do not exceed an addition of 35 % of BOND REPAIR for modifying concrete / mortars

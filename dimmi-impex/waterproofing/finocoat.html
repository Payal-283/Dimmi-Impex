FINO COAT

FINO COAT provides excellent waterproofing characteristics and resistance to algae & fungus. FINO COAT is a water based, durable elastomeric coating that fills & covers hairline cracks and maintains a clean surface. Brush applied waterproofing coating for Balconies, terraces, wet areas & chajjas.

Area of Application

Exterior surfaces of RCC, concrete panels, plastered masonry walls..

Features & Benefits

  • Impermeable to wind driven rain and thus waterproofs
  • Is elastic in nature and covers hairline cracks
  • Higher thickness and thus longer life
  • Algae and fungus resistant
  • Decorative with low dirt pick up
  • Applied along with a special primer to resist efflorescence
  • Non toxic

    • Technical Information

  • Nature: Single component.
  • Appearance: Emulsion (Available in White )
  • Specific Gravity: 1.29
  • Coverage: 35 sq.ft/lt. for 2 coats
  • Coating Thickness: 110 microns DFT for 2 coats
  • Elongation (250 micron DFT): >400% (Typical Value at 30°C)
  • Re-coating period: Min. 5 hrs (at 30°C)
  • Method of application: By brush, roller or spray
  • Adhesion on Cementitious substrate: Excellent
  • Asbestos: Excellent
  • Packaging: Available in 20KG

    • Instructions: Precaution/Storage

  • Keep out of reach of children

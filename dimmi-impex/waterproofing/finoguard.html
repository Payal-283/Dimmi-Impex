FINO GUARD

YANKONIZE FINO GUARD is a high performance two component flexible acrylic polymers modified cement based waterproof coating for concrete and masonry surfaces. The system consists of a powder and a liquid, is grey in colour and easily applied by brush.

Area of Application

In Terrace waterproofing

Mother slab must be sound / mechanically surface prepared / grouted for cracks for repair of existing slab & re-waterproofing, the mother concrete slab must be stripped off its old treatment completely. Mechanical Methods must be adopted to prepare the surface and vacuum suck to clean off all loose particles & debris. In new construction, the terrace slab must be cast to the required slope for effective drain of the rain water. In situations where drain points are provided at one end only, suitable provision must be made to drain off the water. The slab must be thoroughly wetted with water to a state where it is saturated. But extra care must be taken to see that there is no stagnant or standing water. Any such water must be mopped off. As such, the concrete substrate to receive the treatment must be in a saturated surface dry condition, where the slab is thoroughly wet but dry to touch Mix the both the parts of YANKONIZE FINO GUARD with a slow speed (100 - 150 RPM) forced action mixer for big quantity of 15 kg, to achieve an even consistency. Consume the mixed material within 30 minutes at 300C. All the powder lumps formed during mixing must be hand-crushed completely. PLEASE DO NOT ADD WATER during mixing.

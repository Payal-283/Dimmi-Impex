WD FIXO - SH

WD-FIXO Marine is particularly formulated for bonding of furnitureâ€™s, which are exposed to water or places open to high humidity. It is used in Kitchen cabinets, Bathroom shelf etc. where exposure to water is very high.

Area of Application

WD-FIXO can be applied with brush, roller spreader or fingers. The usual procedure to follow is:

  • Clean both the surface to be bonded.
  • Apply one coat of WD-Fixo on both or one surface (as per requirement).
  • Press the two surfaces together and keep it under pressure till adhesives dries completely.

    • Features & Benefits

  • Strong bonding. The bond is so strong that if two wooden pieces are joined with WD-FIXO and hammer is hit heavily, the wood will give way but not the WD-FIXO BOND.
  • Recommended for bonding any two material where at least one of them is porous eg. Paper, cardboard, particle board etc.
  • Other related applications.
